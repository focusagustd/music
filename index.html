Trilogy

16.08.15 "Agust D"

First Mixtape: "Agust D"

At age 23, Min Yoongi revealed his alter ego "Agust D" by releasing his first mixtape via SoundCloud. Critics viewed Agust D as a major departure from his previous catalog as a member of BTS with a hardcore rap style highlighting his underground influences. He has proved his talents in producing and songwriting as a soloist.

20.05.22 "D-2"

Return as a King: "D-2"

Despite being a surprise drop, the title track "Daechwita" and the album "D-2" broke various records when they were released effortlessly. Daechwita was the fastest MV (7hr 51min) by a Korean soloist to surpass 10 Million views (surpassing the previous record held by his another solo song "Interlude:Shadow") on YouTube when it was released. 

23.04.21 "D-DAY"

Worldwide Acclaimed: "D-DAY"

His first official solo album as the final chapter of this trilogy for Agust D, a sort of rebirth from the wrath of his beginnings. The album was sold over 1,000,000 copies on the first day marking the huge success of the rapper. He also became the FIRST and ONLY Asian Act in History to Top the billboard Rap Songwriters Chart.

SOLD-OUT

World Solo Tour 2023

28 concert dates, covering U.S. and Asia, attracted a total attendance of 330,000. All dates were sold out. Gaining the total revenue of over USD 57,200,000, D-DAY Tour is the HIGHEST grossing concert tour by a K-soloist.

He also held the record of the HIGHEST grossing arena concert by a rapper in U.S. history (over $3 million in a single arena concert)
