About

Who is

Agust D?

Rapper Min Yoongi goes by two stage names: SUGA for his group work with BTS, and Agust D for his solo releases. The three identities are as different as their names.

He is a rapper, producer and songwriter. A truly self-made artist.

He combines rap, hip-hop, and trap elements, exploring themes of mental health and self-expression in music. His trilogy of Agust D, D-2 and D-DAY are known for powerful delivery and thought-provoking storytelling.

"Agust D" is a rearrangement of SUGA's name along with a nod to his hometown. "Agus" is Suga spelt backwards while "dT" stands for Daegu Town (his hometown/old band).



2006 — 2010

Start of Music Career

Born in 1996, Min Yoongi (a.k.a. Agust D/SUGA) learnt about MIDI at age of 13 after being inspired by hip-hop groups. Since then, he worked a part-time job at a record studio and was active under the name Gloss as an underground rapper. As part of the hip hop crew D-Town in 2010, he produced "518-062", a song commemorating the Gwangju Uprising (a democracy movement in Korea). He used music to express himself.

Listen to "518-062"

I was getting sick of doing music in Daegu (hometown), so I decided to try out for auditions in Seoul (capital).

2010 — 2013

Trainee

There were three categories for the auditions by Big Hit Entertainment: rap, dance and composition. Yoongi took auditions in rap and composition. After the auditions, Yoongi signed with the company and moved to Seoul to study as a high school student and be a trainee. As his family did not support his music career, Yoongi needed to work as a delivery man to pay high school fees and maintain his living, especially everything was expensive in Seoul.

I had to choose between eating noodles or taking the bus home.

Unfortunately, while doing that job, Yoongi had an accident while delivering the goods, which seriouly injured his shoulder. He had to endure the pain and practised dancing intensively (yes, his company decided to let him debut as part of an idol group with hardest choreography).

2013 — 2016

Debut

In 2013, Yoongi debuted as a member of the South Korean boy band BTS. Besides being the lead rapper of the group, Yoongi also produced, wrote and composed many songs for the group. Therefore, apart from being a rapper, Yoongi is also a producer, a writer and a composer. ​

Agust D (since 2016)

2016

Introduction of Alter Ego: " Agust D "

Yoongi introduced his alter ego "Agust D" in 2016. A darker and raw extension of himself that he hadn’t shared before. Agust D emerged as a standalone artist with his mixtape, where he looked inward and bared forth his battles with depression, fame, anxiety and poverty. ​

The difference between SUGA and Agust D is that there is a lot more that I can openly express and that I can show a more raw side to myself [with Agust D]. What’s similar is that both sing of dreams and hope.

Yoongi used over 7 years to complete his trilogy with 3 full-length albums. He had been preparing for his solo tour since the first mixtape. As expected, both his D-DAY album and D-DAY tour win all reputation from fans and critics around the world. ​
